Instruction

You are the only waiter in this restaurant, can you sever every customer ?

Use up and down arrow keys to waiter and press space to send the dishes. The first customer will always catch the dish and eat it.

You need to serve them before they reach you. If this customer has enough foods, he leaves immediately. Otherwise, he will line up again for another dish of food. But if there is no customer to catch the dish, then it falls on the floor and breaks, and the waiter will lose. The waiter earns tips from customers for each dish he has served.

How many tips that you can get?
